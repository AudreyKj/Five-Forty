ARQUETYPO

*******************

Time is an entity that is choreographing itself. This entity is being us. In a continuous and inventive flow to all possible narratives and all directions from eternity to eternity, from the most distant ancestor to the farthest future, everything belongs to its rhythm.

I suggested, in these drawings, self-choreographic forms that express rhythm, polarity, and transformation. I chose black and white cutouts as an archetypal thought of this dance between days and nights in a harmonious fit.

A rooster crows and ties the day to the night and the undoing of my dream to the making of my day. Everything is made of an impermanent foam that flows eternally for the whole.
