MAGIC PLAN IS A DIGITAL DREAM

...or rather a nightmare spam.

It was created collecting data from codes generated with Unix Time, a descriptive system that counts the seconds that have elapsed since January 1rst, 1970.

The Unix timestamp is a date and time function syntax reference for various programming languages because it can represent a point in all time zones at once. In other words, this system represents time on the digital side of our world.

    I converted 5 different dates at 5:40 am to Unix Time:
  • 05/03/2017 at 5:40 = 1493790000
  • 03/05/2017 at 5:40 = 1488692400
  • 04/05/2017 at 5:40 = 1491370800
  • 02/03/2018 at 5:40 = 1517636400
  • 18/09/2018 at 5:40 = 1537249200


I then use this information to make google searches, which triggered ideas and images that served as an inspiration to find how a dream might perform on my cloud at 5:40 am.

Found quantified patterns made me create this emotionally confusing infographic: it represents the human-technology gap, acting as a digital scroll down a dream.
